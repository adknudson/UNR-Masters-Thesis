In other words we can evaluate a model by answering four questions.

Question One: Domain Expertise Consistency Is our model consistent with our domain expertise?

Question Two: Computational Faithfulness Will our computational tools be sufficient to accurately fit our posteriors?

Question Three: Inferential Adequacy Will our inferences provide enough information to answer our questions?

Question Four: Model Adequacy Is our model rich enough to capture the relevant structure of the true data generating process?
